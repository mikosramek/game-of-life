Conway's Game of Life

Recreated by Miko Sramek

0.25

How to Play:

Use your mouse to click a square - this will add or remove life. Grey means there isn't life, red means there is.

Once you're satisfied with your life arrangements, hit the Play button to see what happens.

Whether life will flourish or die is based on 4 rules:

For each space that has life:

  1. Each cell with one or no neighbors dies, as if by solitude.
  2. Each cell with four or more neighbors dies, as if by overpopulation.
  3. Each cell with two or three neighbors survives.

For each space that has no life:

  1. Each cell with three neighbors becomes populated.
